Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a simple two-player game played on a 3×3 grid. One player is X and the other is O, and they take turns marking empty squares. The first to get three in a row—across, down, or diagonally wins, or it ends in a tie if the board fills up with no evident row pattern.

Memory Card Game

A memory card game is played with all the cards facing down. Players take turns flipping over two cards, trying to find a matching pair. If they match, the player keeps the pair and goes again. If this isn't the case, cards are flipped back and it’s the next player’s turn. This goes on until there are no cards left to be flipped.

Connect Four

Connect Four is a two player game where players take turns dropping colored coins into a grid, where the first player to connect four of their respective colored coins wins the game, allowing you to compete with friends in a strategic game.
